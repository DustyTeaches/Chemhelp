Fun Chemistry Facts

  • 💥 Water expands when it freezes—most substances shrink!
  • ☁️ Clouds are mostly tiny water droplets, not vapor.
  • 🔥 You can boil and freeze water at the same time (triple point).
  • 🧬 DNA is a polymer, like plastic!
  • 🧂 Table salt is made from explosive sodium and poisonous chlorine.
